Royal Enfield recalls Meteor 350

Royal Enfield has issued a recall for select Meteor 350 models, manufactured and sold between December 2020 and April 2021, owing to a defective ignition coil. Find out if your motorcycle is affected. Royal Enfield upped the prices of the Meteor 350 back in April 2021. The hike ranges from Rs 5,000 to Rs 6,000, and the asking price of the base model has gone up from Rs 1,78,744 to Rs 1,84,319 (ex-showroom Delhi). No mechanical or cosmetic change has been made to the motorcycle. If you’re planning on buying the Royal Enfield Meteor 350, check out the waiting period for May 2021 here.
